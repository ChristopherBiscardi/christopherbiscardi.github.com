## Flicks

- 1v1 pros consistently hit 70mph flicks.
- Using bakkesmod hitbox plugin is useful for understanding how car and ball relate with respect to power and positioning

### Tornado Flicks

- Ball has to be really far forward to get powerful flick.
- Holding jump and delaying flick gets you closer to ball and thus more power
